## 2. SHIPS (Complete Fleet)

### **Production Ships** (Passive Income)

#### **Salvage Drone** (Tier 1)
- **Unlocked:** Start
- **Base Cost:** 10 Debris
- **Cost Growth:** 1.15x
- **Production:** 1 Debris/sec
- **Description:** Basic autonomous salvage unit
- **Upgrades:**
  - Efficiency I-V: +20% production each (Cost: 50/150/400/1K/3K Metal)
  - Swarm Protocol: +5% per 10 drones owned (Cost: 500 Electronics)

#### **Refinery Barge** (Tier 1)
- **Unlocked:** Start (after 5 drones)
- **Base Cost:** 100 Metal
- **Cost Growth:** 1.18x
- **Production:** Converts 10 Debris/sec → 2 Metal/sec
- **Description:** Processes raw debris into refined metal
- **Upgrades:**
  - Smelting I-III: Conversion ratio 10→3, then 10→4, then 10→5 (Cost: 500/2K/8K Metal)
  - Throughput Boost: +50% processing speed (Cost: 1K Electronics)

#### **Electronics Extractor** (Tier 2)
- **Unlocked:** GEO orbit
- **Base Cost:** 500 Metal + 50 Electronics
- **Cost Growth:** 1.18x
- **Production:** Extracts 0.5 Electronics/sec from debris field
- **Description:** Salvages functional circuits from wreckage
- **Upgrades:**
  - Component Recovery: +0.3 Electronics/sec (Cost: 300 Electronics)
  - Precision Extraction: Also generates 0.1 Rare Materials/sec (Cost: 2K Metal + 500 Electronics)

#### **Fuel Synthesizer** (Tier 2)
- **Unlocked:** Mars orbit
- **Base Cost:** 1,000 Metal + 100 Electronics
- **Cost Growth:** 1.20x
- **Production:** Converts 20 Metal/sec → 1 Fuel/sec
- **Description:** Synthesizes reaction mass from refined materials
- **Upgrades:**
  - Efficient Synthesis: Ratio improves to 15→1 (Cost: 5K Metal)
  - Quantum Catalysis: Ratio improves to 10→1 (Cost: 50 Rare Materials)

#### **Matter Extractor** (Tier 3)
- **Unlocked:** Jovian System
- **Base Cost:** 50K Metal + 5K Electronics + 500 Fuel
- **Cost Growth:** 1.20x
- **Production:** 1 Rare Material/sec
- **Description:** Extracts exotic matter from gas giant atmosphere
- **Upgrades:**
  - Deep Dive Protocol: +0.5 Rare/sec (Cost: 100 Rare Materials)
  - Atmospheric Filtration: Also generates 5 Electronics/sec (Cost: 10K Electronics)

#### **Quantum Miner** (Tier 4)
- **Unlocked:** Run 7 prestige reward
- **Base Cost:** 1M Metal + 50K Rare Materials + 10K Fuel
- **Cost Growth:** 1.22x
- **Production:** 0.1 Exotic Alloys/sec
- **Description:** Mines exotic matter from quantum fluctuations
- **Upgrades:**
  - Quantum Resonance: +0.05 Exotic Alloys/sec (Cost: 500 Exotic Alloys)
  - Zero-Point Extraction: Also generates 2 Rare Materials/sec (Cost: 1K Exotic Alloys)

### **Active Ships** (Missions & Actions)

#### **Scout Probe** (Tier 1)
- **Unlocked:** After reaching GEO
- **Base Cost:** 500 Metal + 100 Electronics
- **Cost Growth:** 1.18x
- **Function:** Send on missions to discover derelicts
- **Mission Duration:** 10 minutes base (reduced by tech)
- **Discovery Chance:** 15% per mission base (increased by quantity + tech)
- **Fuel Cost Per Mission:** 50 Fuel
- **Upgrades:**
  - Advanced Sensors: +10% discovery chance (Cost: 2K Electronics)
  - Quick Survey: -30% mission time (Cost: 5K Metal)
  - Long-Range Comms: Can scout adjacent orbits (Cost: 500 Rare Materials)

#### **Salvage Frigate** (Tier 2)
- **Unlocked:** After discovering first derelict
- **Base Cost:** 5K Metal + 1K Electronics + 200 Fuel
- **Cost Growth:** 1.20x
- **Function:** Salvage discovered derelicts
- **Mission Duration:** 20 minutes base
- **Success Rate:** 90% base (increases with upgrades)
- **Fuel Cost:** Varies by derelict (50-500 Fuel)
- **Upgrades:**
  - Reinforced Hull: +5% success rate (Cost: 10K Metal)
  - Salvage Efficiency: +25% loot from successful salvages (Cost: 5K Electronics)
  - Rapid Deployment: -40% mission time (Cost: 1K Rare Materials)

#### **Heavy Salvage Frigate** (Tier 3)
- **Unlocked:** Run 3 prestige reward
- **Base Cost:** 100K Metal + 20K Electronics + 5K Fuel
- **Cost Growth:** 1.22x
- **Function:** Salvage dangerous/rare derelicts, 2x speed
- **Mission Duration:** 15 minutes base
- **Success Rate:** 95% base
- **Fuel Cost:** Varies (100-1,000 Fuel)
- **Special:** Can salvage "Hazardous" derelicts (normal frigates fail)
- **Upgrades:**
  - Military-Grade Shielding: 98% success rate (Cost: 50K Metal + 10K Rare Materials)
  - Cargo Expansion: +50% loot capacity (Cost: 20K Electronics)
  - Emergency Warp: Can abort failed missions, recover 50% fuel (Cost: 5K Rare Materials)

#### **Deep Space Scanner** (Tier 3)
- **Unlocked:** Run 5 prestige reward
- **Base Cost:** 500K Metal + 100K Electronics + 20K Rare Materials
- **Cost Growth:** 1.22x
- **Function:** Passive - increases derelict spawn rate
- **Effect:** +2% derelict spawn chance per scanner owned
- **Special:** Reveals derelict rarity before salvaging
- **Upgrades:**
  - Quantum Entanglement Array: +3% spawn per scanner (Cost: 50K Rare Materials)
  - Predictive Algorithms: +10% chance for rare+ derelicts (Cost: 100 AI Cores)

#### **Colony Ship** (Tier 4)
- **Unlocked:** Lunar Orbit + Research
- **Base Cost:** 1M Metal + 200K Electronics + 50K Fuel + 10K Rare Materials
- **Cost Growth:** 1.25x
- **Function:** Establish permanent colonies in orbits
- **Effect:** Colony provides +25% production in that orbit permanently
- **Deployment:** One-time use (ship consumed)
- **Build Time:** 1 hour real-time
- **Limit:** 1 colony per orbit
- **Upgrades:**
  - Colony Efficiency: +40% production instead (Cost: 500K Metal + 100K Rare)
  - Auto-Salvage Bay: Colony auto-salvages common derelicts in orbit (Cost: 1M Metal + 50K Electronics)

### **Orbit-Specific Features**

#### **LEO (Low Earth Orbit)**
- **Theme:** Human debris, satellites, old stations
- **Derelict Types:** Weather satellites, communication relays, ISS modules
- **Common Loot:** Debris, basic electronics
- **Special:** Tutorial messages, safe learning environment

#### **GEO (Geosynchronous Orbit)**
- **Theme:** Military and corporate satellites
- **Derelict Types:** Spy satellites, corporate data centers, military platforms
- **Common Loot:** Electronics, data fragments
- **Special:** Unlock Electronics Extractors, first rare derelicts

#### **Lunar Orbit**
- **Theme:** Abandoned moon bases, mining equipment
- **Derelict Types:** Mining haulers, research outposts, colony ship fragments
- **Common Loot:** Metal, rare materials (trace amounts)
- **Special:** First colony ship blueprints available

#### **Mars Orbit**
- **Theme:** Failed colonization attempts
- **Derelict Types:** Colony ships, terraforming equipment, supply depots
- **Common Loot:** Fuel reserves, life support systems, electronics
- **Special:** Fuel Synthesizers unlock here, first uncommon derelicts common

#### **Asteroid Belt**
- **Theme:** Mining operations gone wrong
- **Derelict Types:** Mining motherships, refinery stations, ore haulers
- **Common Loot:** Massive metal yields, rare materials, AI cores (rare)
- **Special:** First prestige wall, rare derelicts become common

#### **Jovian System**
- **Theme:** Gas giant research stations
- **Derelict Types:** Research vessels, atmospheric harvesters, exotic labs
- **Common Loot:** Rare materials, exotic alloys (small amounts), AI cores
- **Special:** Matter Extractors unlock, epic derelicts appear

#### **Kuiper Belt**
- **Theme:** Edge of civilization, ancient mysteries
- **Derelict Types:** Deep space explorers, ancient probes, alien artifacts
- **Common Loot:** Exotic alloys, AI cores, data fragments, Ark components
- **Special:** Legendary derelicts spawn, most Ark components found here

#### **Deep Space**
- **Theme:** The void beyond, Exodus Fleet main wreckage
- **Derelict Types:** Exodus Fleet capital ships, alien megastructures
- **Common Loot:** Everything at massive scale, final Ark component
- **Special:** Extremely expensive to reach, very rare derelicts, endgame only

### **Derelict Action Choices**

When a derelict is discovered, player chooses:

#### **SALVAGE (Safe)**
- Cost: Listed fuel amount
- Reward: 100% of base loot table
- Success: Guaranteed (or 95% with basic frigates)
- Time: Standard mission duration

#### **HACK (Risky)**
- Cost: Listed fuel + 10% of listed fuel in Electronics
- Reward: 150% of base loot + bonus Data Fragments (10-20% of loot value)
- Success: 85% base (affected by ship upgrades + tech)
- Failure: Lose fuel + electronics, gain nothing, 10% chance ship damaged
- Time: +50% mission duration

#### **DISMANTLE (Slow & Thorough)**
- Cost: Listed fuel × 2
- Reward: 200% of base loot, guaranteed rare+ items, +50% Ark component chance
- Success: 100%
- Time: ×3 mission duration

#### **ABANDON**
- Cost: Nothing
- Reward: Nothing
- Effect: Derelict remains in orbit, can be salvaged later

## 10. PROGRESSION FORMULA & BALANCE

### **Dark Matter Gain Formula**

```
baseDM = sqrt(totalDataFragments / 100)
orbitBonus = 1 + (maxOrbitReached * 0.3)
derelictBonus = 1 + (totalDerelictsSalvaged / 50)
timeBonus = 1 + (hoursPlayed / 10) // diminishes returns for super-long runs

darkMatterGained = floor(baseDM * orbitBonus * derelictBonus * timeBonus)
```

### **Example DM Gains:**

**Run 1:** 500 DF, Asteroid Belt (4), 20 derelicts, 7 days (168 hours)
- baseDM = sqrt(500/100) = 2.24
- orbitBonus = 1 + (4 * 0.3) = 2.2
- derelictBonus = 1 + (20/50) = 1.4
- timeBonus = 1 + (168/10) = 17.8 → capped at 3.0 for balance
- **DM = floor(2.24 * 2.2 * 1.4 * 3.0) = 20 DM**

**Run 5:** 5,000 DF, Kuiper (6), 150 derelicts, 3 days (72 hours)
- baseDM = sqrt(5000/100) = 7.07
- orbitBonus = 1 + (6 * 0.3) = 2.8
- derelictBonus = 1 + (150/50) = 4.0
- timeBonus = 1 + (72/10) = 8.2 → capped at 2.5
- **DM = floor(7.07 * 2.8 * 4.0 * 2.5) = 198 DM**
